Important Questions – Mathematical Foundations

Unit 1 – Indian Knowledge & Basics

  • Explain Panini’s logical structure.
  • Write short notes on Navya-Nyaya Logic.
  • Contributions of P. C. Mahalanobis and C. R. Rao.
  • Applications of logic in computer science.

Unit 2 – Determinants and Matrices

  • Define determinant and its properties.
  • Explain minors and cofactors.
  • Types of matrices with examples.
  • Matrix addition and multiplication.
  • Applications of matrices in real-world problems.

Unit 3 – Statistics

  • Frequency distribution and its importance.
  • Mean, Median and Mode.
  • Variance and Standard Deviation.
  • Applications of statistics in computer science.

Unit 4 – Mathematical Logic

  • Propositions and truth tables.
  • Negation, conjunction and disjunction.
  • Implication and bi-conditional statements.
  • Tautology and contradiction.
  • De-Morgan’s Laws.
