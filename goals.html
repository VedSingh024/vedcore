Data Structures Important Questions

Unit 1 – Basics and Complexity

  • Define Data Structure with classification.
  • Explain linear and non-linear data structures.
  • What is an algorithm? Characteristics of a good algorithm.
  • Explain time and space complexity.
  • Best, average and worst case analysis.

Unit 2 – Arrays

  • Explain one-dimensional and two-dimensional arrays.
  • Write short note on sparse matrix.
  • Advantages and disadvantages of arrays.
  • Applications of arrays in real-world problems.

Unit 3 – Stack, Queue and Linked List

  • Define stack. Explain push and pop operations.
  • Applications of stack (expression evaluation, recursion).
  • Define queue and its types.
  • Explain circular queue and priority queue.
  • Operations on singly and doubly linked lists.

Unit 4 – Trees and Heaps

  • Define tree and explain terminology.
  • Binary tree properties and representation.
  • Tree traversal methods.
  • Binary Search Tree and its operations.
  • Define heap and explain insertion and deletion.

Unit 5 – Graphs, Hashing and Sorting

  • Define graph and explain its representation methods.
  • Explain BFS and DFS traversal.
  • What is hashing? Explain collision handling.
  • Compare different sorting techniques.
  • AVL tree definition and advantages.
